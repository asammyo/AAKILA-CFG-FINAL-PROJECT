How is started

Dr Food & Photos was founded in 2020 during the pandemic as hobby during the final year of my PhD. It began as food, but then the photography took over. My creative passion went to new levels. After the pandemic, however, the pace life picked up back so I decided to create a little space to capture and my creative hobbies of food and photos.
